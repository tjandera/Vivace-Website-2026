★ Aisle 4 · FAQ

Questions at the checkout.

Can't find your answer? DM us on Instagram or grab the floating Ask Us button.

01 When and where is Vivace 2026? +
11 — 13 August 2026, 10:00 to 18:00 daily, at the SMU Concourse (Connexion, level 1). Closing performance on the 13th moves to SMUC Hall.
02 Do I need to register beforehand? +
Walk-ins are welcome — but registering with your matric ID gets you the loyalty card and pre-booked try-out slots for over-subscribed CCAs.
03 How do I sign up for a CCA? +
Tap any CCA card → go to the booth → scan the QR. Audition / try-out CCAs will email you a slot within 48 hours.
04 Can I join more than one CCA? +
Yes — and we encourage it. A typical SMU student fills their basket with two or three. Schedules are coordinated by SSU.
05 Are non-SMU friends allowed? +
The fair is open to the public. CCA membership itself is SMU-only with a few exceptions — check each booth.
06 Where do I redeem partner deals? +
At the partner outlets directly during the event window. Flash matric card + the deal screen from this site.
